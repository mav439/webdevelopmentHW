Chicken Carbonara

Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 package (7 ounces) spaghetti
  2. 8 slices bacon, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  3. 1 medium onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
  4. 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  5. 2 cups cut-up cooked chicken
  6. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  7. 1/2 cup whipping (heavy) cream

Cooking Direction

  1. 1 Cook and drain spaghetti as directed on package.
  2. 2 While spaghetti is cooking, cook bacon in 3-quart saucepan over low heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until crisp. Remove bacon from saucepan with slotted spoon; drain. Drain fat from saucepan, reserving 1 tablespoon in saucepan.
  3. 3 Cook onion and garlic in bacon fat over medium heat about 3 minutes, stirring frequently, until onion is tender. Stir in spaghetti, chicken, cheese and whipping cream. Cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through. Toss with bacon.
